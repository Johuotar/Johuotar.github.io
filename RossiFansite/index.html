Valentino "The Doctor" Rossi is an Italian motorcycle road racer and 9-time MotoGP World Champion. A MotoGP icon, and one of the most famous Italian sportsmen of all time.

Known for his bike number 46, this MotoGP racer from Monster Energy Yamaha MotoGP factory team was born on the 16th of February 1979, in Urbino, Italy.

Rossi is known the world over as “The Doctor”, a nickname given by his father.

Rossi is said to be the most popular MotoGP rider of all time. No other rider has had such an impact on the sport as Rossi.

There is a Monster energy drink named after Rossi, the drinks flavour is refreshingly light and crisp citrus.

Fan merchandice is available, find the best way to cheer for Rossi by visiting the official Valentino Rossi online store!

He is currently contracted to race until the end of the 2020 season. When it ends he will be 41 years old.

Rossi has won MotoGP World Championship 9 times.
